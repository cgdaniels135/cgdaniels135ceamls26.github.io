what_i_learned: |
  Today’s session was heavily dedicated to finalizing the remaining logistics and content for our upcoming mid-summer symposium presentation. We kicked things off by focusing on team organization and explicitly delegating different parts of the presentation to individual group members. By doing this, everyone walked away with a clear understanding of their specific speaking roles and what they are responsible for delivering on the big day. Next, we shifted our attention to our project video, which had previously taken us quite a while to piece together. We spent time deliberating on exactly which components and clips of our live demo were the most crucial to include to best showcase our hard work. After successfully finalizing the video, we transitioned into a live practice run to test our flow. We presented our complete material in front of two other lab groups to simulate the real event environment. This mock presentation gave us a great feel for what the actual symposium will feel like and highlighted exactly how our audience will interact with our content.
  
blockers: |
  It was highly challenging to consolidate all of our presentation content and findings into the strict 20-minute time frame allotted for the session.

reflection: |
  Today was a highly productive session focused on refining our final presentation for the upcoming mid-summer symposium. We successfully established clear roles for the team, assigning specific sections of the presentation to each member so everyone knows exactly what they are responsible for delivering. Additionally, we finalized our project video after carefully deliberating on which key elements of our live demo would best highlight our work. To build confidence and simulate the actual event environment, we did a run-through and presented our material in front of two other lab groups. This practice run provided invaluable insight, though it also revealed a major blocker regarding our pacing and time management. We found it quite difficult to consolidate our comprehensive research and technical findings to fit strictly within the required 20-minute time frame. Moving forward, our primary focus will be trimming the fluff and streamlining our transitions to ensure our narrative remains impactful yet concise.
